## Pricing Architecture

Devise has a two-tier pricing structure designed to land fast and expand as the customer's AI footprint matures.

### Tier 1 — Startups & Teams

**$9 per user / month.** Self-serve. Up to 200 employees.

Includes:
- Full Devise Engine (Understand · Act · Quantify)
- All four intervention types (Tool Steering, Workflow Discovery, Coaching, Friction Removal)
- 6,500+ AI tools detected and monitored
- macOS and Windows desktop sidekick
- Team dashboard and ROI reports
- Slack & Microsoft Teams delivery
- SSO ready

Land motion: download → install → see your team in 60 seconds → ROI in first session.

### Tier 2 — Enterprise

**Custom per-user / year + platform fee.** 200+ employees.

Includes everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Network connectors (Zscaler, Netskope, Palo Alto, CrowdStrike, F5, Checkpoint)
- HRIS integration (Workday, BambooHR, Rippling)
- SSO & SCIM provisioning
- Data residency (US, EU, on-prem)
- Dedicated CSM, custom SLA
- Compliance package (SOC 2 reports, HIPAA BAA, GDPR DPA)
- Board-ready reporting
- Optional regulated-industry add-ons (PHI detection for healthcare, SEC/FINRA audit trails for financial services)

Land motion: enterprise sales → security review → SSO/SCIM + 1 network connector → org-wide rollout in 90 days.

### Pricing Philosophy

Simple pricing. Built to prove its own ROI. No usage tiers, no hidden meters, no "talk to sales for any meaningful feature." The product proves its value before the contract is signed because the AI Opportunity Assessment is delivered on day zero.

The boldest sales claim: "Devise pays for itself in license recovery before you ever count time saved."

## Revenue Model Components

1. **Subscription (primary)** — Per-seat ARR. Self-serve floor is high-margin and compounding; enterprise contracts are multi-year with annual price escalators.
2. **Platform fees (enterprise)** — Connector provisioning, data residency, dedicated SLA, BYOK.
3. **Compliance & vertical packages** — Add-ons for HIPAA BAA, SEC/FINRA audit trails, EU AI Act readiness, ITAR-compliant hosting.
4. **Marketplace expansion (future)** — Workflow distribution between customers (opt-in), benchmark licensing, anonymized cross-industry insights.

### Unit Economics — Why This Works

- **Land cheap, expand fast.** $9/user/month for SMB is below the line where IT needs to approve.
- **Each new customer makes the engine smarter.** Cross-company role benchmarks reduce CAC for the next cohort.
- **The measurement loop is the moat.** Churning means losing the ROI proof to the board.
- **Connector pull-through.** Each enterprise connector becomes a sales channel.

## Why AI Adoption Matters — The Urgency Argument

The 30-Day AI adoption path is critical because timing is the real challenge for organizations:

**The case for acting now:**
- AI is already embedded in daily work, product roadmaps, and strategic planning. The debate about its value is over.
- The real risk is not that AI won't work — it's that competitors will operationalize it faster.
- Every day without visibility is a day of unmanaged risk, wasted license spend, and missed productivity.
- Shadow AI spreads at the same pace as the tools themselves. By the time a manual audit finds it, the exposure has compounded.

**The 30-day rollout framework:**
- **Days 1–10:** Visibility. Map the AI landscape. Understand who uses what.
- **Days 11–20:** Policy enforcement. Steer to sanctioned tools. Block risky workflows. Start coaching.
- **Days 21–30:** Measurement. Prove the before/after delta. Present the first ROI report to leadership.

Organizations that follow this cadence move from "uncertain AI usage" to "defensible, governed adoption" in under a month. Organizations that don't follow it continue accumulating risk, waste, and missed opportunity every single day.
