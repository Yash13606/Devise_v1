## 2. What Devise Is

Devise runs a continuous, closed loop in three phases:

### Phase 1 — Understand
Detect every AI tool used across the org. Map who uses what, how often, for what tasks. All 6,500+ tools tracked through network-layer signals — no browser extensions, no SDK instrumentation, no per-endpoint agents.

### Phase 2 — Act
Four intervention types that turn passive data into measurable outcomes:
1. **Intelligent Tool Steering** — When someone uses a suboptimal tool, Devise nudges them to one the org already pays for.
2. **Workflow Discovery & Distribution** — Finds high-performing AI workflows employees built organically, packages them, distributes to similar roles.
3. **Personalized AI Coaching** — Micro-coaching based on each person's actual usage, role, and seniority. Not generic training.
4. **Friction Detection & Removal** — Spots struggling employees (high abandonment, repeated attempts, tool-switching) and intervenes with specific fixes.

### Phase 3 — Quantify
Devise owns the entire measurement loop. Observe the before state. Perform the intervention. Observe the after state. Calculate the delta. Outputs:
- Time savings, hours recovered per week, attributed to specific interventions
- License optimization (redundant licenses identified, unused seats activated)
- Adoption velocity (% of workforce effectively using AI over time)
- Workflow multiplication (distinct AI use cases per employee)
- **Salary-calibrated ROI** — every metric grounded in loaded costs by role, seniority, and location. "200 hours saved for legal" also means "$47,000 in value."

## 3. The Devise Behavioral Engine

Six subsystems, sequenced from observation to outcome:

1. **Team Mapping** — Reconstructs the org chart in under 60 seconds using professional intelligence from 800 million profiles. No CSV upload. No HRIS integration required.
2. **Tool Detection** — Network-traffic pattern recognition across 6,500+ AI tools. Sees ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Copilot, Cursor, Midjourney, Notion AI, Perplexity, Grammarly, Replit, v0, Bolt, Lovable, Windsurf, and the long tail of shadow AI.
3. **Behavioral Analysis** — Jobs-to-be-done inference. From job title, seniority, and industry, Devise infers each person's specific tasks. A Senior Financial Analyst does predictable things. Devise knows on day zero.
4. **Intervention Engine** — Real-time nudges, coaching, and steering delivered through the desktop sidekick, Slack, or Teams.
5. **Outcome Measurement** — Before/after instrumentation tied to each specific intervention. Causal chain is never broken.
6. **Loop Optimization** — Every cycle is smarter. More data, better nudges, faster coaching. As the installed base grows, cross-company benchmarks make the engine sharper for everyone. A true data network effect.

## 9. Strategic Thesis

**Dashboards report. Devise intervenes.**

The market is moving in three waves and Devise sits at the intersection of all three:

1. **Visibility wave** — Companies need to *see* their AI usage. Most tools today only see what's instrumented (SDK-first). Devise sees everything via the network.
2. **Adoption wave** — Seeing is not enough. The 95% pilot-failure rate is a behavior problem. Devise is the only system that *acts* on what it sees.
3. **Accountability wave** — Boards and regulators are asking "what value did AI deliver?" Devise owns the measurement loop end-to-end, which is the only way to answer that honestly.

**Workforce-first beats agent-first.** Most enterprise AI risk today is not from autonomous agents — it's from ordinary employees using powerful tools in ordinary ways. You cannot secure agents you do not yet have. You can secure the people who are already using AI every day. Devise starts where the risk and the value actually are.

## 10. Why Now, Why Devise

- **Network-layer is the only complete view.** SDK-first observability misses anything not pre-instrumented. Browser extensions miss desktop apps. Self-reporting decays. Devise reads the network — every AI tool, no exceptions.
- **Professional intelligence as cold-start solution.** 800M-profile data unlocks day-zero org mapping and jobs-to-be-done inference. Other tools need months of customer data before they're useful; Devise is useful in 60 seconds.
- **Data network effect.** Every new customer benchmarks better for the next. Each new role observed across the install base sharpens the engine for every customer.
- **The measurement moat.** Because Devise both performs the intervention AND measures the delta, it can prove causal ROI. Dashboards that only measure adoption at a point in time cannot. This is the structural advantage.
