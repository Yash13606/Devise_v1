# 02 — Business Model

## Pricing Architecture

Devise has a two-tier pricing structure designed to land fast and expand as the customer's AI footprint matures.

### Tier 1 — Startups & Teams

**$9 per user / month.** Self-serve. Up to 200 employees.

Includes:
- Full Devise Engine (Understand · Act · Quantify)
- All four intervention types (Tool Steering, Workflow Discovery, Coaching, Friction Removal)
- 6,500+ AI tools detected and monitored
- macOS and Windows desktop sidekick
- Team dashboard and ROI reports
- Slack & Microsoft Teams delivery
- SSO ready

Land motion: download → install → see your team in 60 seconds → ROI in first session.

### Tier 2 — Enterprise

**Custom per-user / year + platform fee.** 200+ employees.

Includes everything in Tier 1, plus:
- Network connectors (Zscaler, Netskope, Palo Alto, CrowdStrike, F5, Checkpoint)
- HRIS integration (Workday, BambooHR, Rippling)
- SSO & SCIM provisioning
- Data residency (US, EU, on-prem)
- Dedicated CSM, custom SLA
- Compliance package (SOC 2 reports, HIPAA BAA, GDPR DPA)
- Board-ready reporting
- Optional regulated-industry add-ons (PHI detection for healthcare, SEC/FINRA audit trails for financial services)

Land motion: enterprise sales → security review → SSO/SCIM + 1 network connector → org-wide rollout in 90 days.

## Revenue Model Components

1. **Subscription (primary)** — Per-seat ARR. The self-serve floor is high-margin and compounding; enterprise contracts are multi-year with annual price escalators.
2. **Platform fees (enterprise)** — Connector provisioning, data residency, dedicated SLA, BYOK.
3. **Compliance & vertical packages** — Add-ons for HIPAA BAA, SEC/FINRA audit trails, EU AI Act readiness, ITAR-compliant hosting.
4. **Marketplace expansion (future)** — Workflow distribution between customers (opt-in), benchmark licensing, anonymized cross-industry insights.

---

## Unit Economics — Why This Works

- **Land cheap, expand fast.** $9/user/month for SMB is below the line where IT needs to approve. The desktop sidekick deploys in under 60 seconds.
- **Each new customer makes the engine smarter.** Cross-company role benchmarks reduce CAC for the next cohort (better demos, sharper ROI projections from day zero).
- **The measurement loop is the moat.** Because Devise instruments before/after, retention is structurally high: churning means losing the ROI proof to the board.
- **Connector pull-through.** Each enterprise connector (Zscaler, Microsoft Entra ID, Salesforce) becomes a sales channel — "your team already uses X; here's what we surface on top of it."

## The Customer Lifecycle

1. **Awareness** — Buyer reads the workforce-first AI governance argument or sees a peer benchmark.
2. **Demo** — 30-minute walkthrough tailored to their stack. Devise shows what it sees for orgs like theirs.
3. **Pilot install** — Sidekick on a department or network connector to a single region.
4. **Day-zero report** — AI Opportunity Assessment + Shadow AI audit delivered in 5 minutes.
5. **First interventions activate** — Tool Steering and Friction Detection run automatically. Quick wins quantified.
6. **Expansion** — Org-wide rollout, additional connectors (SIEM, HRIS), compliance pack.
7. **Board-ready** — Monthly/quarterly ROI report exported to CFO and Board. Renewal is implied.

## Pricing Philosophy

> Simple pricing. Built to prove its own ROI.

We deliberately keep the pricing page legible — no usage tiers, no hidden meters, no "talk to sales for any meaningful feature." The product proves its value before the contract is signed because the AI Opportunity Assessment is delivered on day zero.

The pricing also enables our boldest sales claim: *"Devise pays for itself in license recovery before you ever count time saved."*
