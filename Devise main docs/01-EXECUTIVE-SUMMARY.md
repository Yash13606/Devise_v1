## Why Devise Wins

1. **Network-layer is the only complete view.** Competing observability tools depend on SDK instrumentation, browser extensions, or self-reporting. They miss everything not pre-wired. Devise reads the network and sees every AI tool.
2. **The cold-start problem is solved.** Most behavioral analytics tools take months to be useful while they accumulate customer data. Devise plugs into 800M professional profiles on day zero and produces a complete AI Opportunity Assessment in 5 minutes — the kind of analysis consultants charge six figures for.
3. **Devise owns the measurement loop end-to-end.** It performs the intervention *and* measures the delta. That causal chain is what lets us prove ROI in dollars. Dashboards that measure adoption at a point in time cannot.
4. **The data network effect compounds.** Each new customer's roles, tasks, and workflows sharpen the engine for every other customer. The gap widens with scale.
5. **Workforce-first beats agent-first.** Most AI risk and value today live in everyday employee usage, not in autonomous agents. We start where the dollars actually are.
