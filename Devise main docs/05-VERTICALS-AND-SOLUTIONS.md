## Segment 5 — Technology (SaaS, Platform, Developer-Heavy Orgs)

> **Your engineers use 8 AI tools each. You pay for 3. Find the other 5.**

### What Devise Shows on Day One
- 47 AI tools across the org → 15 licensed, 32 shadow → $340K of overlap waste.
- Cursor + Copilot + ChatGPT → same job, 3× the cost.
- ChatGPT + Claude + Gemini → content generation, 3 licenses.
- Notion + Grammarly + Perplexity → writing & research, pick one.

### The Gap — Why Devise Pays For Itself in Tech

| Top 10% of Engineers | Everyone Else |
|---|---|
| 3 AI tools (focused) | 6 AI tools (scattered) |
| 8 workflows per week | 2 workflows per week |
| 12 hrs saved per week | 2 hrs saved per week |
| Ship 2.1× faster | No measurable impact |

**The difference isn't talent. It's which tools they use and how.** Devise identifies what works and spreads it across the org.

### Engineer-Trust Posture
- Does *not* read code or prompts.
- Observes AI tool usage and analyzes interaction patterns to enforce policies, detect sensitive data, and steer engineers to approved tools.
- Works with IDE-based AI tools (Copilot, Cursor) via network signals.
