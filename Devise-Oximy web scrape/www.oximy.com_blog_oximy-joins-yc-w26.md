# Oximy Joins Y Combinator W26: Building the System of Record for Enterprise AI

We're coming out of stealth and joining Y Combinator's Winter 2026 batch. Here's why we're building the visibility layer the enterprise has been waiting for.

Jan 27, 2026·4 min read

![Oximy joins Y Combinator W26](https://www.oximy.com/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fblogs%2FYC.png&w=3840&q=75)

![](https://www.oximy.com/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fnew_home%2Fhorizontal_double_line.png&w=3840&q=75)

Today, we're excited to announce that Oximy is joining Y Combinator's Winter 2026 batch and coming out of stealth.

We're building the **System of Record for enterprise AI usage**.

KEY TAKEAWAY

Enterprise AI adoption has outpaced visibility. Organizations are deploying AI everywhere, but most cannot answer basic questions: Who is using what? What data is being shared? What does it cost? Oximy answers these questions.

## The Problem

AI tools are everywhere in the enterprise. Employees use browser extensions, desktop apps, and unmanaged accounts across dozens of providers. Product teams embed models into customer-facing workflows. Platform teams experiment with autonomous agents.

Most of this happens without IT visibility. Without governance. Without a clear picture of cost or risk.

This isn't a future problem. It's happening right now, in every enterprise, across every department.

## What We're Building

Oximy provides complete visibility into AI usage across the organization:

- **Pulse** \- Real-time adoption metrics showing exactly how AI is being used across teams, departments, and applications
- **Spend** \- Cost consolidation that brings fragmented AI expenses into a single view
- **Oversight** \- Governance controls that enforce security policies and surface compliance gaps before they become incidents

We map AI activity across every browser and desktop app in the company. No surveys. No guesswork. Just data.

## Why Y Combinator

Joining YC W26 gives us access to the network, resources, and expertise needed to move fast on a hard problem. The enterprise AI visibility gap is widening every day. We're not waiting.

We're also grateful to be backed by **Seven Stars, Schema VC, SF1, Gokul Rajaram, and Timeless Ventures** \- investors who understand that the infrastructure layer for enterprise AI is being built right now.

## What's Next

The window to solve this problem is narrow. AI adoption isn't slowing down, and the visibility gap only grows wider.

If you're a CIO, CISO, or IT leader ready to stop guessing and start seeing, we want to talk.
