# The Hidden Cost of Shadow AI

Why Employee AI Usage Is the Largest Unpriced Risk in the Enterprise. A deep analysis of Shadow AI as a distinct enterprise risk vector driven by human behavior.

### Executive Summary

Shadow AI refers to the use of AI tools within an organization without the knowledge or approval of IT or security teams, often driven by employees seeking immediate productivity gains.

Unlike traditional enterprise software adoption, Shadow AI proliferates through human behavior. This paper argues that Shadow AI is a distinct enterprise risk vector that cannot be adequately addressed through policy alone.

The costs associated with Shadow AI are often unmeasured and under-recognized - but they are real, pervasive, and growing.

### Key Findings

- →8 out of 10 employees report using unauthorized AI tools at work
- →68% of employees use free-tier AI tools via personal accounts
- →57% of personal account users input sensitive corporate data
- →90%+ of employees share confidential data with AI despite knowing risks
