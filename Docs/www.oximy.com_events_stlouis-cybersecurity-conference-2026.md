# St. Louis Cybersecurity Conference

Join Naman Ambavi, CEO of Oximy, keynoting at the St. Louis Cybersecurity Conference hosted by FutureCon. Learn how security leaders can enable AI adoption while maintaining control and visibility.

![St. Louis Cybersecurity Conference - Image 1](https://www.oximy.com/_next/image?url=%2Fimages%2Fevents%2Fstlouis-2026.png&w=1200&q=75)

Location

Hilton St. Louis Frontenac

Keynote

How to Say Yes to AI (Without Losing Control)

Every CISO faces the same pressure: the business wants AI everywhere, yesterday. The instinct is to slow it down, lock it down, approve one tool, and block the rest. But that approach is failing. In this keynote, Naman shares what separates CISOs who are stuck playing defense from those who've become AI enablers for their organizations, and introduces a visibility-first framework that lets security say "safe yes" — governing AI usage without blocking productivity.
